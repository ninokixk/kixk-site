What We Do

01

Email Security Audit

We assess and enforce SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records across your domain. Misconfigured email is the entry point for invoice fraud and impersonation attacks.

02

Perimeter Defence

External port scanning and asset discovery to identify exposed remote access services — RDP, FTP, SMB — before attackers do. Run post-consent from a hardened VPS.

03

Credential Leak Audit

Domain-level breach analysis via HaveIBeenPwned and DeHashed. We identify which staff credentials are circulating in known breach datasets and act on them.

04

Monthly Retainer

Ongoing monitoring, quarterly phishing simulations, MFA compliance checks, and a one-page PDF report your insurer will accept. Delivered on a 12-month engagement basis.
